About Orchestrix

Orchestrix is an open-source orchestration platform by AbateG, designed to help teams manage projects, tasks, and resources efficiently. Built with modern .NET technologies and best practices, it’s ideal for organizations seeking a robust, extensible, and production-ready solution.
